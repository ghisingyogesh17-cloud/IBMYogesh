The Legacy of Freedom

Their Sacrifice was Not in Vain

The heroic sacrifice of Shukra Raj Shastri, Dharma Bhakta Mathema, Dashrath Chand, and Gangalal Shrestha sparked a political awakening across Nepal. Their executions in 1941 united the public against the century-old autocratic Rana regime, leading to its ultimate collapse just a decade later in 1951.

Today, they are remembered as the foundational pillars of our nation's freedom. Every democratic right we enjoy today stands on the brave sacrifice they made for our future.

Remembering the Heroes

Every year, Nepal celebrates Martyrs' Day on January 30 (Magh 16) to honor their contribution to our freedom. Statues of the four martyrs are built at Shahid Gate (Martyrs' Gate) in Kathmandu, keeping their memory alive for generations to come.

Honor and respect to the Martyrs of Nepal.
